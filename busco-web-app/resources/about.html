Who We Are

Driving the Sweet Success of the Philippine Sugar Industry

BUSCO Sugar Milling Co., Inc. stands as a pillar of industrial strength in Butong, Quezon, Bukidnon. Established to harness the rich agricultural potential of the region, we have grown into one of the country's premier sugar milling facilities.

With a relentless commitment to modernization, our production capacity now exceeds industry standards, processing thousands of metric tons of cane daily. We bridge the gap between local farmers and the national market, ensuring quality sugar reaches every Filipino household while uplifting our local community.

Our History

From a bold vision in Bukidnon to a national leader in sugar production, trace our journey through the decades.

1980

Foundation

BUSCO was established in Butong, Quezon, Bukidnon, recognizing the immense potential of the region's soil for sugarcane cultivation.

1995

Expansion Phase

Major infrastructure upgrades doubled our milling capacity, allowing us to serve more farmers across the province.

2010

Technological Modernization

Implementation of automated processing systems improved sugar recovery rates and operational safety standards.

Present Day

Sustainable Leadership

Today, BUSCO is a leader in sustainable milling, community development, and consistent Quedan performance.
